Honey Garlic Soy Glazed Salmon



DESCRIPTION

A quick and easy dinner that delivers! This is honey garlic soy salmon, a dish made from just 6 ingredients in under 35 minutes. Marinate fresh salmon in a 5-ingredient sauce, and then reduce the rest down on the stove to a glaze as the salmon bakes. Serve with thickened glaze, brown rice, and your favorite vegetables for a simple, flavorful, and healthy meal.

INGREDIENTS

  • 680g Salmon
  • Honey
  • Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • Garlic, fresh or minced
  • Fresh Ginger

METHOD

  1. Place salmon in a container or zipped-top bag. Mix the marinade ingredients together and pour about half over the salmon.
  2. Marinate the salmon for at least 15 minutes and up to 4 hours. Stretch this to 8 hours if needed.
  3. Place salmon on lined baking sheet. Reserve used marinade if desired to reduce with the rest of the unused marinade. The sauce will caramelise over the salmon as it bakes
  4. While the salmon bakes, reduce the remaining marinade on the stove to make a glaze. You want a rich, thick glaze.
  5. Drizzle the glaze all over the warm baked salmon. You could even drizzle a spoonful onto the salmon fillets towards the end of the bake time and return them to the oven to finish baking.
  6. Feel free to garnish the honey garlic soy salmon with green onion and/or sesame seeds.

The edges are crisp, the honey garlic soy salmon is tender, and the flavors are unbelievable!
